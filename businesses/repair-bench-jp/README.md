# `repair-bench-jp` — Repair Bench JP: Hardware Repair YouTube Channel

YouTube + TikTok channel: buy broken laptops/consoles/phones from Hard-Off and Akihabara (¥500–¥2,000), film the repair. Unique angle: Japan-sourced junk electronics that Western repair channels can't access. Revenue via AdSense, Amazon JP affiliate links (tools, parts), and channel memberships/Patreon.
